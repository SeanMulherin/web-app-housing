Forcasting Selling Price of Houses in the U.S.

March 20, 2024

Hi team,
The data we will be using is generously provided by Zillow. We will look at the average sale price of houses by city and month. We have montly data for all months since 2000. The goal is to fit a forcasting model to predict the trend for cities of personal interest; we will utilize the Prophet Model to do so. I've taken the liberty to complete some initial exploratory data analysis as shown above.

Most notably, I've created an interface for you to input your city and forecast length so that you can observe and explore the forecasted trends and prediction metrics computed by the Prophet model. This model is notorious for its accuracy and flexibility. Housing prices are atypically volatile so this model does particularly well at producing accurate home price forecasts.
